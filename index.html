What Student Council Does

From community events to school-wide traditions, Student Council helps make Duxbury High School a more connected, spirited, and student-centered place.

Amplify Student Voice

Represent the ideas, concerns, and perspectives of students in conversations about school life, climate, and culture.

Build School Spirit

Organize events and traditions that bring our Dragon community together and celebrate what makes DHS unique.

Lead Through Service

Plan fundraisers, service projects, and initiatives that support classmates and the broader Duxbury community.
