About Student Council

Learn more about how Student Council serves Duxbury High School through student voice, spirit, and leadership.

Our Mission

The Duxbury High School Student Council exists to amplify student voice, foster a strong sense of school spirit, and develop leadership in every grade. We work to create inclusive events and traditions that reflect the needs and interests of the student body.

Through collaboration with students, staff, and the community, Student Council strives to make DHS a place where all Dragons feel seen, supported, and connected. We believe that when students lead, our school grows stronger.

By organizing service projects, fundraisers, and celebrations, we aim to model responsible leadership and give back to the greater Duxbury community.

Constitution & Structure

The Student Council follows a written constitution that outlines its purpose, roles, elections, and procedures. This guiding document helps ensure that our work is fair, transparent, and student-centered.
